What we build

XR Training & Simulation

We design and develop extended reality environments for hands-on technical training. Our simulations replicate high-stakes processes—from semiconductor fabrication to emergency response—so learners build competence before touching real equipment.

AI-Driven Adaptive Learning

Our platforms use artificial intelligence to meet learners where they are. Real-time performance data shapes content delivery, pacing, and feedback—creating personalized pathways that respond to each user's needs, pace, and goals.

Spatial Computing & Immersive Design

We build spatial interfaces that transform how people interact with information. From 3D data visualization to interactive environments, our designs make abstract concepts tangible and navigable.

Civic & Public Impact Technology

We partner with municipalities and public institutions to create technology that strengthens the connection between governments and the communities they serve—accessible tools for civic engagement, public health, and resident services.
