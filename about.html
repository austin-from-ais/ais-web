Austin Stanbury

Founder

Austin Stanbury is a scholar, developer, and artist working at the intersection of extended reality, artificial intelligence, and public impact. As CEO & Co-Founder of Augmented Info Systems, he leads the design and development of immersive platforms that make complex knowledge accessible, interactive, and real.

His work spans AI-driven pedagogy, spatial computing, and civic technology—building tools that serve semiconductor technicians in simulation, neurodivergent learners navigating new skills, and cities connecting with their residents.

Austin is affiliated with the University of Florida, where his research explores how emerging technology can reshape education and public systems.
